## ⚙️ How it works:
1. **Ingest**: Paste any raw text, subtitles, or notes below.
2. **Digest & Organize**: Move the valuable info into their appropriate structural notes:
   - [Push.md](push.md) (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps, etc.)
   - [Pull.md](pull.md) (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts, etc.)
   - [Legs.md](legs.md) (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves, etc.)
   - [Nutrition & Supplements.md](nutrition%20&%20supplements.md) (Diets, macros, vitamins, creatine, etc.)
   - [Training Principles & Methodology.md](training%20principles%20&%20methodology.md) (Rest times, progressive overload, RPE/RIR, etc.)
   - [Food/](food/) (Food lists, recipes, regional dishes)
3. **Archive**: Once digested, move the raw note or processed source file into the **`.archive/`** directory. It will be hidden from Obsidian and skipped by Git.
